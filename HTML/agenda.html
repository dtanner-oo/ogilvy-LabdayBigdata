Countdown to the Lab Day


Lab Day Agenda

The day itself is built around three key pillars - addressing critical areas of potential, and the fears that could prevent their adoption.

  1. A Smarter World
    Graceful automation or intimidating robotics?

    Data can make the world smarter, safer and fairer - reinventing our definition and expectations of society. Data can make traditional processes work better - work as they should. But is the automation that promises such progress truly benign?
  2. Richer Experiences
    The power of immersion or the fear of invasion?

    A look at how can data enrich our stories, our newspapers, our advertising - and even the objects we hold and want and use everyday. Is our future an internet of things - or an internet of threats?
  3. Human Understanding
    Is to be understood to be served - or controlled?

    Through data, brands, governments and people have the chance to be closer than ever before, promising a level of service that verges on the telepathic. But how do we navigate the privacy issues that dominate this space? And is hyper-individualised marketing and government even the right tactic?

Data is the biggest force for change since the Industrial Revolution - reinventing institutions and accepted processes. But will it be a force for good or bad? Realised through invasive marketing or tailored services? Will our future be made of electric sheep, or connected, empowered individuals? Come and add your voice to the data-debate.

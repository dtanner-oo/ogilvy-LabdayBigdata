6th June 2013

Countdown to the Lab Day


Lab Day Agenda

  • Introduction, Registration and Welcomes (08:45 - 09:30)
  • Keynote Speech (09:40 - 10:00)
    Matt Bayfield - Big Data: Why Now?
  • Section 1 - A Smarter World (10:00 - 11:15)
    where we consider
    the power of data to make the world a better, smarter, more automated place - and the barriers that prevent it being harnessed effectively

    Speakers include:
    Luke Nicholson
    Ordnance Survey
    Liri Anderson
  • Section 2 - Immersive Stories (11:30 - 13:00)
    where we consider
    how data can enrich stories and restructure the way we communicate - and the need to avoid the immersive becoming invasive…or simply ignored

    Speakers include:
    Blippar
    Google
    BBC
  • Lunch (13:00 - 14:00)
  • Section 3 - Human Understanding (14:00 - 15:20)
    where we consider
    the ways in which data can deepen our understanding of humans and social institutions - and the potential this holds for changing behaviour…or manipulation

    Speakers include:
    Richard Seymour
    Science Rockstars
    Tre Azam
  • Section 4 - BIG Discussions and Decisions (15:35 - 17:00)
    where we consider
    the challenges that BIG Data poses to the relationships between people, government and brands, explored through a panel debate. This is followed by Rory Sutherland’s unique perspective on data and behaviour - inspired by the Sevenoaks Car Park

    Speakers include:
    Rory Sutherland
    David Wiener
    Kathryn Corrick
  • Close and Networking Drinks (17:00 - 19:00)
    with thanks to our drinks sponsors RadiumOne
